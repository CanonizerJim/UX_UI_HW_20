RESEARCH:

This project was aimed at creating an app to help social workers find housing for the clients they serve. I interviewed three active social workers and discovered they spend too much time doing administrative busywork and not enough time in one-on-one interactions with their clients.

PROBLEM STATEMENT: 

Social workers are spending too much time and energy on finding beds for clients. As a result, they are burning out from spending too much time on overly manual administrative busywork.
